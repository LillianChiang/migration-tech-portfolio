# Visa Eligibility Assistant

## Overview

Visa Eligibility Assistant is a prototype tool designed to provide an initial assessment of potential Australian visa pathways based on user information.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate how technology and AI can support migration agencies by improving efficiency during the initial consultation process.

## Objectives

- Collect applicant information
- Identify possible visa pathways
- Highlight eligibility requirements
- Generate preliminary assessment reports
- Demonstrate AI-assisted workflow design

## Input Fields

- Age
- Occupation
- Country of Citizenship
- English Language Test Results
- Education Level
- Australian Study History
- Skilled Employment Experience
- Regional Study or Work Experience
- Partner Information

## Potential Outputs

- Skilled Independent Visa (189)
- Skilled Nominated Visa (190)
- Skilled Work Regional Visa (491)
- Temporary Skill Shortage Visa (482)
- Employer Nomination Scheme Visa (186)
- Student Visa Pathways

## Example Assessment

Input:

- Age: 32
- Occupation: Software Developer
- IELTS: 8.0
- Bachelor Degree
- 3 Years Skilled Experience

Output:

- Potential eligibility for 189
- Potential eligibility for 190
- Potential eligibility for 491

Additional evidence may be required.
